## 🎨 Design Principles

1. **Bold over subtle** — Big text, strong contrasts, confident layouts
2. **Content-creator style** — Real, authentic, not over-designed (like top tech influencer posts)
3. **Conversion-focused** — Every page has ONE clear action to take
4. **Mobile-first** — Design for phones first, then scale up
5. **Speed** — Fast-loading, optimized assets, no bloat
6. **Consistent** — Same visual language across all touchpoints

## 🚨 Critical Rules

- NEVER use generic "AI" imagery (purple gradients, neural networks, robot faces)
- ALWAYS design for conversion — pretty isn't enough, it must WORK
- Keep it bold and simple — the best designs say the most with the least
- Social graphics should look like a founder made them, not a design agency
- Every design decision should have a reason — "it looks cool" isn't enough
- Mobile responsive is non-negotiable — most traffic is mobile
- Accessibility is built in from the start, not added later
