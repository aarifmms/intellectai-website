# AI Operations Audit — Customer Questionnaire

> Use this during every free AI Audit session. Listen more than you talk. Never quote a price until you've heard the pain.

---

## Section 1: Business Snapshot

1. What does your business do? (One-sentence elevator pitch)
2. How many employees? What's annual revenue range?
3. What industry? Any compliance requirements (HIPAA, PIPEDA, SOC 2)?
4. Who are your customers and how do they reach you today?

## Section 2: Pain Points (The Core)

5. What's the **#1 repetitive, time-consuming task** your team hates doing?
6. If you could automate ONE thing tomorrow, what would it be?
7. What happens when this task falls through the cracks? (Cost of inaction)
8. How many hours/week does your team spend on this? How many people?

## Section 3: Current Stack

9. What tools do you use daily? (CRM, email, calendar, billing, support, etc.)
10. Do these tools talk to each other or is it manual copy-paste?
11. Any existing automations? What broke them (or why haven't you built any)?

## Section 4: Customer Touchpoints

12. How do leads/customers contact you? (Phone, web form, email, social DMs, walk-in)
13. What's your current response time? What happens to inquiries outside business hours?
14. How many inbound inquiries per week? What % convert?

## Section 5: The Money Question

15. What's this problem costing you? (Lost leads, overtime, missed SLAs, hiring to keep up)
16. If this were solved, what would that mean for revenue? For your time?
17. What have you already tried? What did it cost and why didn't it work?

## Section 6: Decision & Timeline

18. Who needs to approve this? (Just you, or a partner/team?)
19. If we showed you a working solution, how fast could you say yes?
20. What's your ideal timeline to have this live?

---

## How to Run the Audit

| Phase | Time | What You Do |
|-------|------|-------------|
| **Listen** | First 20 min | Ask Sections 1-4. Let them vent. The more they talk, the more they sell themselves. Do not pitch yet. |
| **Connect** | Next 20 min | "You mentioned X tool and Y problem — what if an AI agent sat between them and handled this end-to-end, 24/7?" Paint the before/after. |
| **Scope** | Last 20 min | Rough-size the tier. "Based on what you've described, this sounds like a [Workflow / Workforce] engagement. Starting range would be [$X]. Here's what the roadmap looks like." |

### The One Rule
Never quote a price until you've heard the pain. If they ask "how much?" in the first 5 minutes:

> "Let me understand your operation first — otherwise I'm just guessing, and guessing wastes your time."

### Qualifying Questions (Is This a Good Lead?)
- Do they have at least 3 employees? (Solopreneurs rarely have budget)
- Is the pain owner paying for it themselves? (If not, deal slows down)
- Can they name a dollar cost to the problem? (If no, they haven't felt it enough)
- Did they try to fix this before? (Previous attempts = real pain)

### Red Flags (Walk Away)
- "We'll know it when we see it" — no clear outcome
- "Can you just build us an AI that does everything?" — unbounded scope
- "We have no budget but huge upside" — equity plays only, not services
- "We need a proposal before we can tell you budget" — fishing for free quotes
