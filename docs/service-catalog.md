## Tier 1: AI STRATEGY SESSION — $497

- 60-minute live strategy call (Zoom, recorded)
- Written AI Action Plan (3-5 pages) within 48 hours
- Top 3 automation/AI opportunities ranked by ROI

- Timeline: Call within 3-5 business days

- Timeline: 10-14 business days
- Examples: Appointment booking agent, website chatbot, AI receptionist
