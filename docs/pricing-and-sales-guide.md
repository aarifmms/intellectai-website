## Project Classification Guide

Use these real projects to anchor pricing conversations. The question is never "can we build it?" — it's "which tier does this fall into?"

### Tier 1: AI Workflow Automation ($2,500+)

**What it is:** Connect existing tools with AI-powered decision logic. Automate specific workflows end-to-end.

**When to quote this:**
- Client has clear, bounded workflows (booking, routing, notifications)
- 2-5 tool integrations needed
- No custom agent development required
- Scope is well-defined

**Example conversation starter:**
> "This sounds like a Workflow Automation engagement. We'd connect your CRM, calendar, and email so an AI layer handles scheduling, follow-ups, and routing automatically. Starting range is $2,500. Live in 1-2 weeks."

---

### Tier 2: Custom AI Workforce ($5,500+)

**What it is:** Custom-built AI agents (voice + text) that work autonomously. Multi-agent systems with reasoning, tool use, and escalation logic.

**When to quote this:**
- Client needs voice agents, sales agents, or multi-agent systems
- Complex integrations (CRMs, databases, APIs, calendars)
- Multi-step reasoning required
- 24/7 autonomous operation

**Example conversation starter:**
> "This is a Custom AI Workforce build. We'd design and deploy autonomous agents that handle calls, leads, scheduling, and ops — running 24/7, integrated with your entire stack. Starting range is $5,500. Live in 3-4 weeks."

## Pricing Psychology

### Why $2,500 and $5,500?

- **Below $2,000** — SMBs question quality. "If it's that cheap, does it actually work?"
- **$2,500-$7,500** — The SMB sweet spot. Feels like a serious investment without board approval.
- **$7,500-$15,000** — "This is a real project" territory. Decision-maker needs to be in the room.
- **Above $15,000** — Enterprise SMB or multi-decision-maker sale. Longer cycle, higher close value.

### What Not to Do

- **Don't charge hourly** — Rewards slow work, caps your income. Always project or value-based.
- **Don't lead with price** — If they ask "how much?" in minute one: "Let me understand your operation first — otherwise I'm guessing."
- **Don't discount without removing scope** — "I can do $5,000 if we descope the voice agent and focus on workflow automation." Never just drop the price.
- **Don't skip the audit** — The free audit is your conversion engine. No audit, no quote.
